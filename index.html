Connect your Telegram bot

You will need a Telegram account and a Telegram bot to communicate with your AI assistant. Create yours in 2 minutes, then paste the token below — we'll wire it up automatically when your instance launches.

Don't have Telegram yet? Download it free: iPhone · Android · Desktop

  1. Open Telegram and search for @BotFather
  2. Send /newbot — pick a display name, then a username ending in bot (e.g. Display name: Aimee, Username: aimee_bot)
  3. BotFather replies with your token, e.g. 7123456789:AAExxxxx… — copy it and paste it in the Bot Token field below
