How It Works

Up and running in minutes

From sign-up to autonomous AI agent in four simple steps.

01

Create Your Account

Sign up with your email or Google account. Choose your plan and we'll provision your dedicated cloud environment instantly.

02

Connect Your Tools

Authorize your apps via OAuth — Gmail, Slack, Notion, GitHub, Salesforce, and more. Your claw gets the keys it needs; you stay in control.

03

Personalize Your Claw

Tell your claw about your role, routines, communication style, and priorities. The more context you give, the smarter it operates from day one.

04

Let It Work

Chat with your claw over WhatsApp, Telegram, Slack, or the web app. Set it loose on tasks and watch it handle the busywork so you don't have to.
